Health

The next time you are about to take a scooter....

Think about it.

  • "Do I really need to take a scooter, or can I walk?"
  • "I would be getting better exercise if I walk and it's a lot healthier."
  • "I could afford to spend my money a little smarter."

Walking is good exercise. For those of you who do not like working out, like going to the gym or going for a run, this is a great way to get some steps in while going to class everyday!
