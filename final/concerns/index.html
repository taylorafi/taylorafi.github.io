Health

Everyone has been in these college kids' shoes.

The majority of a college kids' day to day activities include:

  • sleeping,
  • studying and doing school work,
  • stressing out about classes,
  • eating junk food,
  • and, of course, drinking alcohol.

That does not leave much room for physical activity and their primary source of moving around becomes their walks across campus to class. However, e-scooters are actively eliminating what's left of any sort of work out for students.

In a study published by the U.S. Department of the Interior Office of Occupational Safety and Health, researchers found that individuals can burn about nine times more energy by walking to their destination than they would riding a scooter.
