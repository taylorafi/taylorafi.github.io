Safety

The joy in this new mode of transportation has caused electronic scooter users to overlook some major issues that these scooters could bring about.

Electric scooters may seem fun, but they have been causing a noticeable number of accidents. The Swedish Transport Administration conducted a study in Stockholm from May 2019 to August 2019 using datasets from reported injuries to Folksam Insurance and the Swedish Traffic Accident Data Acquisition database (STRADA).
